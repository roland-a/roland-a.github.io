Projects

Infinibiome

Page

This is the first large project I have created. It is a mod for the game Minecraft that adds infinitely varied landscapes. Most other terrain mods I tried only add in multiple predefined biomes. These predefined biomes tend to have little variations within and did not seamlessly transition between biomes. I wanted to make a terrain generator that makes every area feel unique and seamless. This is why I created Infinibiome. Instead of predefined biomes, every world feature such as amplitude, slope, temperature, humidity, etc., are varied and independent.

Unaverage Tweaks

Page

This is another Minecraft mod I have created. Normal Minecraft without any mods tends to feel unpolished. There are a lot of small features missing that I feel would enhance the gameplay greatly. In Unaverage Tweak, I attempt to add every feature I feel is greatly needed. Of course, not every other player who wants to use this mod might agree with everything, so I made every feature configurable and disableable.
